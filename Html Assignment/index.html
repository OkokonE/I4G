COCONUT RICE (My Favourite Meal)

Coconut rice is typically prepared by soaking white rice in coconut milk or cooking it with coconut flakes. Though coconut rice is found in many cultures of the world, it is believed to have its origins in the South Asia, Southeast Asia and Latin America regions.

Ingredients

SN Ingredient Quantity
1 Rice 3 cups
2 Coconut oil 1 tbspn
3 Coconut milk 400ml
4 Water 1.5 cups
5 Onions 1 medium size
6 Tomatoes 3 medium size
7 Bay leave 2 leaves
8 White pepper 1 tspn
9 Chili pepper 1 tspn
10 Fresh thyme 4 sprigs
11 Crayfish 2 tbspn
12 Stock cube 2 pieces
13 Salt To taste

*tspn - teaspoon, tbspn - tablespoon

Steps To Prepare Meal

  • Finely chop the onions, chop tomatoes into large chunks and set aside.
  • Mince chili pepper and set aside.
  • Place a cooking pot on medium heat. Add in 1 tbsp of coconut oil, add the onion and cook until translucent and fragrant.
  • Add in tomato, minced pepper and spices then stir. Cover and leave to simmer on medium heat for 10 minutes.
  • Add in coconut milk and (1.5) cups of water into the tomato mix then stir. Allow to simmer. Taste and adjust for seasoning.
  • Add in washed rice. Reduce heat to low heat.
  • Place a sheet of foil over the pot. Place the lid over it and properly seal the edges with foil (this is a very important step). Set a timer for 30 minutes and allow to cook. Do not open the pot until time is up.By covering the pot with foil & then a lid, you are able to get away with using less liquid. The rice grains are gently steamed & infused instead of being boiled. End result is perfectly separate & delicious grains .
  • Serve coconut rice with Peppered meat or chicken.
